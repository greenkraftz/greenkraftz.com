ABOUT US

It has always been our mission to change lives through meaningful travel, one destination and one experience at a time.

AKZONE is a venture by team of Three young international students with innovative ideas and understanding of the nitty gritties involved in study abroad process. What is special about the team is that two of  partners have studied from different universities abroad and have first hand experience in right from choosing the University to passing out of that University.

They are well aware of the general challenges faced at every stage and can tackle them efficiently and correctly.  AKZONE fills the information gap between students/job hunters with a desire to travel abroad and universities/companies offering international programs.

The team has been successfully providing education consulting services in the Ukraine and Russia since 2013 and now wishes to leverage this experience towards students in India to help them fulfill their dreams and achieve their goals in country and universities of their choice. We bring to the table self-experience in application process, market knowledge, financial status of target countries, and knowledge of funding organisations.

 

BASIC SERVICES

We offer the following services to Indian students interested in an education and jobs abroad.

Dedicated visa advisor

We would get you an admission letter from the University you had applied through us, we can help you with your visa application. You can get visa-specific advice from our advisor who has worked for many years in the visa office. Our dedicated visa advisor will help you with your visa application and visa interview.

Test preparation classes (applicable only for English Speaking Countries)

We conduct free IELTS and TOEFL training classes for students who are looking to sit for these exams. Students based in Chennai, Chhattisgarh, Orrisa, West Bengal, and north eastern states can sign up for in-class training sessions, which are held every week in our offices in Chennai, Raipur, Bhubaneswar, Kolkatta and Guwahati respectively. Students who live in other parts of the country can register for our online training sessions. The classes, which are held six days a week, are conducted by experienced and BRITISH council accredited tutors.

SOP guidance

The Statement of Purpose (SOP) is the most important part of your application. It gives you an opportunity to impress the institution's admissions team and convince them of your qualities as a student. We can help you structure your SOP and refine it based on the university's requirements.

You can access our online blogs and sample SOPs to get a fair idea on the general format. Our advisors will guide you on the specifics of the SOP.

Once you've written your first draft, our team of editors will go through it and suggest changes, if necessary. They will also edit your SOP to make it more presentable. By using our resources, tools and guidance, you can put together an essay that will make you stand out from the crowd.

Pre-departure services

To help you settle into your new place, we will take you through your accommodation options abroad.

Our pre-departure services include helping you with your travel plans and giving you advice on post-study work rights and job prospects. We can tell you what to pack and what not to, for your study destination.

We offer end-to-end services and are gaining the trust of students and their parents as efficient study abroad consultants like assistance in
